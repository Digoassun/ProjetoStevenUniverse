Intro

The Crystal Gems are the remnants of an intergalactic army one day led by Rose Quartz, Steven's mother. Their names are Garnet, Amethyst, Pearl and, their youngest member and still in training, Steven (being the only half-human member).
There are two key goals for the Crystal Gems, the Earth protection against the Diamonds (dictators of the Gems' Homeworld) and Stevens' protection, who lost his mother at birth, inheriting her gem. However, the boy proves himself many times being able to help their experienced mentors and dealing with problems of magnitudes much greater than he is aware of.

The Game

The game will take place inside each character's room. These rooms are magical, having as entry access for all, a single door that contains the gems of Cystal Gems carved into a star.
Each room aesthetically represents the personality of each of the characters. However, everything changes when Rose's gem shines and it make changes to the other rooms, creating different challenges that you, player, will help our heroines to overcome.
